## Verification Rules

1. **Only verified steps count.** Strava, Garmin, Apple Health, or equivalent proof required. Screenshots must show date, step count, and duration.

2. **Every tier counts.** A 10K First Light adds 10,000 steps. A 100K Beat Midnight adds 100,000. The counter doesn't discriminate â€” it just grows.

3. **One entry per person per day.** You can't log a 50K day as five separate 10K entries. Your highest verified tier for that day is what counts.

4. **No retroactive logging.** Steps count from the day you join the community forward. We're walking to the sun together, starting now.

5. **Step length is standardised.** 0.8m per step across the board. Individual stride length varies, but consistency matters more than precision for a collective tracker.

6. **Planetary distances use closest approach.** Venus at ~39M km, Mars at ~55M km. Orbital positions vary, but we use the most optimistic number â€” because optimism is on brand.
