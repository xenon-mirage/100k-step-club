Think about the most steps you've ever done in a day. 10,000? Maybe 20? Now multiply that by ten. That's 100K. A full day on your feet. Most people hear that number and laugh. A few hear it and start doing the math. This is for the ones doing the math.

The Challenge

What is this

Walk 100,000 steps in a single day. That's roughly 80 kilometres (50 miles) before midnight.

A global community doing it together. Monthly events. Verified steps only. No shortcuts, no team splitting, no treadmills.

You pick your distance. You walk it. You prove it.

The Progression

Start at 10K. Earn your way up.

10K

First Light

"I could keep going."

~8 km (5 mi). Most people have done this without trying. Now do it with intent.

25K

In The Flow

"My body found a rhythm."

~20 km (12 mi). Half-marathon distance on foot. Your rhythm locks in. Your mind goes quiet.

50K

Heating Up

"This is where most people stop."

~40 km (25 mi). The decision point. Your body wants to stop. This is where you find out who you are.

75K

Enter Night

"Everything hurts. The sun went down. I'm still walking."

~60 km (37 mi). Sun's down. Legs are screaming. Every step is a choice you keep making.

100K

Beat Midnight

"I finished. I don't have words for it yet."

~80 km (50 mi). The clock is the opponent. 100,000 steps before it runs out. Most won't make it.

The Process

How it works

  1. 1

    Pick your tier

    10K to 100K. Be honest about where you are.

  2. 2

    Train

    We'll help. Prep guides, weekly builds, shoe advice. Your feet will thank you.

  3. 3

    Join the community

    Connect with other big steppers. Learn a thing or two. Share a thing or two.

  4. 4

    Walk on event day

    Wherever you are in the world. Same day. Same challenge.

  5. 5

    Verify

    Strava, Garmin, Apple Health. Screenshot your proof. No proof, no badge.

  6. 6

    Earn your badge

    Your tier. Your name on the wall. Steps added to the collective counter.
